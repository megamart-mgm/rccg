Who We Are

About Winners Assembly Malmö

A parish of The Redeemed Christian Church of God, raising a family of believers who live victoriously through Christ, in the heart of Malmö.

Our Mission

Winning Souls, Building Lives

To reach the city of Malmö and beyond with the uncompromised Word of God, making disciples who worship in spirit and truth and live out the victory that is already theirs in Christ.

Our Vision

A Family for Every Nation

To be a home in Malmö where people of every background and nation find belonging, grow in faith, and are equipped to carry the light of Christ into their homes, workplaces, and communities.

Our Ministries

There's a place for you to serve, grow, and belong. Here's where our ministries are today.

Youth

Raising a bold generation of young people rooted in faith, purpose, and community.

Children

A safe, fun, Christ-centered space where our children learn God's Word at their own level.

Women's Ministry

A sisterhood of prayer, fellowship, and growth, encouraging women in every season of life.

Men's Ministry

Building men of integrity, faith, and purpose who lead well at home and in the community.

Music / Worship

Leading the church into God's presence through anointed, excellent, spirit-led worship.

Outreach

Taking the love and Gospel of Christ beyond our walls, into Malmö and the wider community.
