You Are Welcome Home

A family where everybody belongs,
and every victory is shared.

Winners Assembly Malmö is a parish of The Redeemed Christian Church of God, planted in the heart of Malmö to raise a community of believers who live victoriously through Christ. Whether you are visiting Sweden, newly settled in Malmö, or have called this city home for years — you have a seat waiting for you here. Come as you are, and leave transformed.

"...we WIN together in Christ"

Romans 8:37

What We Stand For

Four Pillars of Our Assembly

Powerful Worship

Encountering God in spirit and truth, through anointed praise and worship every service.

Spirit-Filled Community

A warm family across Malmö where relationships are built and no one worships alone.

Life-Changing Teaching

Sound, practical biblical teaching that equips you to live victoriously in everyday life.

Victory in Christ

Standing on God's promises, we live as more than conquerors through Him who loves us.
